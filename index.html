Global AI Adoption & Workforce Impact

Built a full-scale, 4-module machine learning pipeline on 150,000+ company records (10,000 unique companies across 9 industries and 6 regions) to analyze AI adoption stages, business performance, and workforce transformation.

  • 📊 Module 1 — Adoption Classifier: XGBoost multi-class model to predict AI adoption stage (none/pilot/partial/full) — Macro F1: 0.83, Weighted F1: 0.89, ROC-AUC: 0.98
  • 🧠 Module 2 — Maturity Segmentation: K-Means (k=4) clustering segmented companies into AI Leaders, Steady Adopters, Struggling Experimenters, and Early-stage Laggards; top cluster shows 3.6× revenue growth vs. bottom

  • ⚖️ Module 4 — Ethics & Risk Analysis: OLS regression with 14-variable confounder set revealed reverse causality — ethics committees are a response to risk, not a cause of it
